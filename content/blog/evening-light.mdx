Overhead lighting is for offices. Homes deserve better.

## Three lamps, one room

Aim for three light sources at three heights: floor, hip, and bench.
Each in the range of 2400–2700K. The eye reads warmer pools of light
as evening; the body follows.

## The bulb matters more than the lamp

A beautiful shade with a cool LED inside is a small tragedy. We ship
every Homeera lamp with a matched bulb so the first night feels right.

## Dim, don't switch

Add a dimmer. Then stop turning lamps off — turn them down. Rooms
shouldn't fall off a cliff at bedtime.
