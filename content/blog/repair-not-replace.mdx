A chair that lasts thirty years is not a discount — it is the whole point.

## The repair tag

Every piece arrives with a small linen tag. On it: the maker's name,
the year, and a code that opens a repair ticket on this site. No proof
of purchase. No warranty fine print.

## What we cover

Anything we made. Loose joints, frayed seams, tarnished brass that
went too far. We pay for shipping one way; you pay the other.

## What it costs

Materials at cost, labour at workshop rates. Most repairs come in under
forty dollars. The point is not the price — it is the option.
